Tips to Conserve More Water

*When washing dishes instead of having the water on non-stop grab a large plactic basket and put the soaped dishes inside so when it is full you may rinse all the soapy dishes at once.

*When brushing your teeth only have the water in for when you need to rinse.

*Try to take less baths and shorter showers

*Don't flush random things down the toilet, dispose of your unfinished cereal somewhere else

*As fun as they are water fights are very wasteful so it's best to limit them as much as possible
